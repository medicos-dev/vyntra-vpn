Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Is Vyntra really free?

Absolutely! Vyntra's core protection and connectivity features are completely free forever. We believe privacy should be accessible to everyone. While we may introduce optional premium features in the future, the essential VPN functionality will always remain free.

Do I need an account?

No account required! Simply download the app, open it, and tap connect. We've designed Vyntra to work instantly without any sign-up process, email verification, or personal information collection.

What devices are supported?

Currently, Vyntra is available for Android devices. We're actively working on expanding to iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux. Follow our updates to be notified when your preferred platform becomes available.

What data do you store?

We store only the minimum data necessary to provide reliable service, such as basic connection logs for troubleshooting. We do not monitor, log, or store your browsing activity, personal data, or online behavior. Your privacy is our priority.

How fast is Vyntra?

Vyntra uses smart routing to automatically select the fastest available server near your location. While VPN speeds depend on various factors, we optimize our infrastructure to minimize speed impact and provide the best possible performance.

Is Vyntra safe to use?

Yes, Vyntra uses industry-standard encryption to protect your connection. We implement modern security protocols and regularly update our systems to ensure your data remains secure and private while browsing.
